Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image resizer work?

Our tool uses browser-based JavaScript to process your images. When you upload an image, we create a canvas element to resize it according to your specifications without sending the data to any server.

Is there a limit to the image size I can upload?

The limit depends on your device's memory and browser capabilities. Most modern browsers can handle images up to 10-20MB without issues.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens in your browser. We don't store your images on any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Currently, our tool supports single image processing. For batch processing, you would need to resize each image individually.
