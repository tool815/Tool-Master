Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image converter tool

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may show ads to support the service, but all features are available without payment.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

You can convert images up to 20MB in size. For larger files, we recommend resizing them first before conversion.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Currently, our tool supports single image conversion. For batch processing, you'll need to convert each image individually.
